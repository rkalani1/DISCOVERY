DISCOVERY Study Recruitment

Eligibility Criteria

SCREEN (Inclusion) – all must be YES:

  • [ ] Age ≥18
  • [ ] Hospital admission with acute ischemic stroke (AIS), intracerebral hemorrhage (ICH), or aneurysmal subarachnoid hemorrhage (aSAH)
  • [ ] Radiographic confirmation of stroke type
  • [ ] Able to complete baseline visit within 6 weeks of onset
  • [ ] Able to provide informed consent (self or proxy)
  • [ ] Fluent in English or Spanish prior to stroke

EXCLUDE if any are TRUE:

  • [ ] Pre-stroke dementia or failed dementia pre-screen
  • [ ] Enrollment in a conflicting non-approved study
  • [ ] Unable to complete protocol due to advanced directives or severe comorbidities

Bedside Message for Eligible Patients

“Please consider participating in the DISCOVERY study, which looks at how stroke affects thinking and memory over time. Taking part will not change or delay your medical care. If you join, you will see a stoke neurologist at Harborview Medical Center’s Stroke clinic within the next six weeks, and that visit will include brief thinking and memory tests along with a small blood draw—about 25 milliliters (around five teaspoons). Follow-up would occur again around 3–6 months and in 18 months, with a short phone call each year. Would you be open to hearing more or talking with our research coordinator?”
